Bhagavad Gita – Chapter 17 (பகவத்கீதை – அத்யாயம் 17)

The value of Sraddha is highlighted both at the beginning and at the end of this chapter. Therefore, it is called Sraddhatrayavibhaga-yoga.

The summary of Bhagavad Gita – Chapter 17, given by Swami Paramarthananda, is available for download.
